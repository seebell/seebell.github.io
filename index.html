About Me

Hi, I'm Sibel. About my hobbies, I love going to the theme parks, reading books, watching movies, playing boardgames and videogames, going to the beach, spending time with my cat Lord Voldemort and my dog Joker, playing mini golf and bowling. Originally, I'm from Turkey; moved to Key West, Florida in June 2013 for a training program and decided to stay afterwards. After 4 years in Key West, I got bored of small island life and moved to Orlando; which was my favorite vacation spot because of the theme parks.

About my career background; I had experience in different fields. After getting my BA degree in English Literature, I started my career as English teacher at language schools in Turkey. After I moved to Florida, I made a change and got involved with F&B Management, then continued with sales and hospitality. However, I've always regretted about not studying web development since that field was much more interesting for me. So I've decided to switch my career into coding and web development, and hopefully attending Coding Bootcamp will help me get the job that I've always wanted.
